Gold & Silver

Priced off a live global spot rate (troy oz → gram, converted to ₹ at the live USD/INR rate) — this is the closest reliable free proxy available. It runs roughly 10–15% below Aura Gold's own price, since that includes import duty, GST and making charges that a global spot rate doesn't. Works without connecting a broker.
